Projects

Road Lane Line Detection

A computer vision project to detect lane lines in real-time using OpenCV library. First converted the image into gray scale image and used Gaussian Blur to reduce the noise and smoothen the image. Canny edge detection to identify the sharp changes in intensities of edges. Finally applied the Hough Transform technique to identify straight lines in the region of interest.

Tea Leaf Disease Prediction

Tea leaves' diseases caused by constant exposure to pathogens leads to significant crop yield loss globally. Diagnosing the tea leaf disease at an early stage minimizes the tea yield loss. To solve this problem image processing is the best option to detect and diagnose the disease. The main goal is to develop an image processing system that can identify and classify the tea leaf disease.

Movie Recommendation

Two types of systems which I used to predict the recommendation system are Content Based and Collaborative Filtering. A Content-Based system provides recommendations directly based on the similarities of items. Whereas Collaborative Filtering systems recommends about the interests of a user by collecting preferences or taste information from many other users. In the collaborative system I used the memory based collaborative filtering technique.

Prediction of Subscribers to the Term Deposit.

Term deposits are the major source of income for a bank. The task was to predict if the client will subscribe to the term deposit or not. First I performed Exploratory data analysis on the dataset provided and finally I built the logistic regression model for linear data and for non linearity I used Decision Tree and finally evaluated how accurate my prediction was.
